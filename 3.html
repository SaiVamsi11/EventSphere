GMRIT Hosts ACM India Regional Chapter Summit

GMRIT Campus proudly hosted the prestigious ACM India Regional Chapter Summit on October 18, 2024, bringing together technology leaders, academicians, and industry pioneers from across the country for a day of knowledge sharing and collaboration.

Summit Highlights

  • 200+ delegates from 35 institutions across India
  • Keynote addresses from ACM India leadership
  • Panel discussions on emerging technologies
  • Chapter best practices workshop
  • Networking sessions with industry partners

The summit featured thought-provoking discussions on artificial intelligence, quantum computing, and the future of computer science education in India. GMRIT's ACM student chapter members played an active role in organizing the event and moderating sessions.

This landmark event positioned GMRIT as a leading institution for technology discourse in the region and established valuable connections with ACM chapters nationwide. Plans are already underway to make this an annual event.
