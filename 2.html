GMRIT ACM Student Chapter Outreach Session at GRIET

On December 3rd, 2023, the ACM Student Chapter of GMRIT, Hyderabad successfully conducted an outreach session at Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology (GRIET), fostering inter-institutional collaboration and knowledge sharing.

Key Achievements

  • 85+ participants from GRIET's computer science department
  • Technical workshop on modern software development practices
  • Panel discussion featuring GMRIT ACM executive members
  • Live coding session demonstrating problem-solving techniques
  • Q&A round about ACM membership benefits

The event began with an introduction to ACM's global initiatives by our chapter president, followed by technical workshops on competitive programming. GRIET students actively participated in hands-on coding challenges guided by our team.

The session concluded with networking opportunities and plans for future collaborative events, including a proposed joint hackathon in 2024. Feedback from participants showed 94% satisfaction with the session content and delivery.
