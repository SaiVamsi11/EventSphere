Empowering Future Innovators: GMRIT ACM Hosts Hour of Code

The ACM Student Chapter of GMRIT, Hyderabad made programming accessible to all students through our Hour of Code event, part of a global movement reaching tens of millions of students in over 180 countries.

Event Highlights

  • 150+ participants from various engineering disciplines
  • Beginner-friendly workshops using Scratch and Python
  • Industry insights from guest speaker Ms. Priya Sharma (Google Developer Expert)
  • Interactive coding challenges with prizes for top performers
  • Networking session with ACM executive members

The event commenced with an inspiring talk by our Computer Science HOD, Dr. Rajesh Kumar, who emphasized how computational thinking benefits all engineering disciplines. Participants then engaged in hands-on coding activities guided by ACM student mentors.

Feedback showed 92% of attendees felt more confident about coding after the event, with many signing up for ACM's advanced programming workshops. The success of this event has paved the way for making Hour of Code an annual tradition at GMRIT.
