About Us

Unleashing Innovation. Create Impact

Our Mission

ACM-GMRIT is dedicated to fostering technical growth and innovation among students. We aim to bridge the gap between academia and industry by organizing workshops, hackathons, and networking events.

Our chapter provides a platform for students to enhance their technical skills, collaborate on projects, and connect with industry professionals.

We believe in learning by doing and encourage hands-on experience through various technical and non-technical events throughout the year.

What We Do

We organize a variety of events including technical workshops, coding competitions, hackathons, guest lectures, and networking sessions.

Our flagship events include annual hackathons, tech symposiums, and coding marathons that attract participants from across the region.

We also conduct regular study groups, project showcases, and career development sessions to help students prepare for their professional journeys.
